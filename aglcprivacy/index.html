AGLC Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right. This privacy policy explains how the AGLC Citation Generator handles your data and protects your privacy.

Data Collection and Storage

The AGLC Citation Generator is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All citation data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • No tracking cookies are used to monitor your activity
  • No user accounts or registration is required

Local Storage

When you save citations, they are stored in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your citations remain on your device
  • They persist between browser sessions until you clear them
  • They are not accessible to other websites or users of your device
  • They will be lost if you clear your browser data or use private/incognito mode

Third-Party Services

The AGLC Citation Generator uses the following third-party services:

  • Flatpickr: A lightweight date picker with no tracking capabilities
  • Particles.js: A visual background effect with no data collection
  • Google Fonts: For typography (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Font Awesome: For icons (subject to Font Awesome's privacy policy)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
